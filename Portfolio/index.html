About Me


Nice to meet you! I'm Andrea Trotta, a passionate developer with some experience in the world of programming and software development. Over the past few years, I've had the opportunity to explore various technologies and deepen my knowledge in different areas. In the field of web development, I've learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and have a basic understanding of JavaScript. I've worked on small web projects, constantly seeking to improve my skills and experimenting with new ideas.

Regarding software development, I have a basic knowledge of languages like C, C++, and Python. I've had the opportunity to contribute to small-scale projects and learn from more experienced colleagues along the way. I'm also quite comfortable in the Linux environment and use it as my primary operating system for my daily work.

I'm fascinated by the world of cybersecurity, an interest that has accompanied me since childhood. While I'm aware that I still have much to learn, I'm motivated to grow and deepen my knowledge in this field. I'm open to new learning opportunities and excited to connect with other technology enthusiasts to share experiences and knowledge.

Thank you for taking the time to read my brief introduction. I'm eager to see where my journey in the world of technology will take me!
